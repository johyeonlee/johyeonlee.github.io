CIRCLE Mobile Application
Personal Project | Aug - Sep 2023

| BRIEF
"Social world beyond the screen."
Meeting people who share similar goals with you and stay motivated.

| ROLE
Visual Design, Research, Wireframe, Prototype

| TOOLS
Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator

The idea behind this application is to create a platform where individuals can connect with others who share similar goals and interests, providing a space for mutual support and motivation. By creating a platform where users can connect with like-minded individuals, we aim to provide a supportive environment where they can share experiences, offer guidance, and motivate each other towards achieving their aspirations. Whether it's pursuing fitness goals, advancing in a career, or exploring personal interests, our application serves as a virtual community where individuals can find encouragement, inspiration, and camaraderie.

By facilitating these connections, I hope the users may overcome challenges, celebrate successes, and embark on fulfilling journeys of personal growth and development.
