DHL Website Renewal
Personal Project | Dec 2023 - Jan 2024

| BRIEF
Redesigning and branding an existing corporate website as a modern responsive website

| ROLE
Research, Content Strategy, UI/UX Design, User Testing

| TOOLS
Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator

After graduation, I immersed myself in refining my user experience design skills while reconnecting with my family and relishing my time in South Korea. One project that stood out as particularly stimulating was the redesign of the DHL website, where I could apply my skills in a practical setting while blending my passion for design with real-world challenges.

As an international student during my time at university, I frequently utilized DHL's international package delivery services for shipments from my family in Korea and friends in various countries. Given my, and my friends' frequent interactions with the website, I was inspired to modernize the current DHL website with a fresh new look.

"What's up with the huge images?

... I'm not using any business related services. Then the only information useful for me is the top small Track Your Shipment section I guess."

- Friend 1

"These two links lead to the same page.

... There are links that appear both in the middle and the bottom of a page. Isn't it redundant to display same information twice even it needs to be highlighted?"

- Friend 2

My journey began upon discovering deficiencies in the current UI, including a cluttered layout lacking clear sections, outdated design with huge images, and the presence of multiple duplicate links on a single page. These issues resulted in a disjointed user experience, making navigation confusing and impeding access to desired content. This led me to create a more intuitive and efficient browsing experience for users.

Lessons & Takeaways

Looking back, I'm grateful for how the beginning of the year served as a catalyst for my growth as a designer. At times, it felt overwhelming due to the abundance of information on the website. However, overcoming these challenges allowed me to expand my understanding beyond design and visual elements, emphasizing the importance of effectively conveying meaningful information to users.

The five-month period following graduation offered me a harmonious blend of professional development and personal fulfillment, liberating me from the rigors of university life and enabling me to fully immerse myself in various endeavors. I eagerly anticipate continuing to evolve and embrace new challenges in the journey ahead.
